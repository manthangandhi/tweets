2. How We Use Your Data

  • To send you curated, darkly witty tweet drops via email.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate DarkTweetBot™:

4. Unsubscribe

Every email includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time, and your email will be marked as unsubscribed in our system.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure. We never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.

6. Contact

If you have questions or requests regarding your data, please contact our concierge at support.
